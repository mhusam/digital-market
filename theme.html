● FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can't find what you're looking for? Drop us a note at [email protected].

Where does Cortex get its data?

From your connected systems — Salesforce, HubSpot, your email and calendar, your call recordings. We don't sell your data, we don't train shared models on it, and you own every byte.

How long does onboarding take?

The first connectors take under an hour. Most teams are running live sequences within an afternoon. Enterprise rollouts with custom security review usually wrap in 2–3 weeks.

Which LLMs do you use?

A mix of frontier and fine-tuned open-source models, routed per task to balance cost, latency and quality. You can opt into US-only or EU-only inference at any tier.

Is Cortex SOC 2 compliant?

Yes — SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, ISO 27001 and HIPAA available on Enterprise. Request our trust report from inside the product or at trust.cortex.ai.

Will it replace my CRM?

No. Cortex sits on top of Salesforce or HubSpot and makes them better. The CRM remains your system of record — Cortex just stops your reps having to feed it by hand.

What does "pay per meeting booked" mean?

You only get billed when a Cortex-sourced lead actually books a sales meeting in your calendar. No meeting, no charge — no seat fees regardless of headcount.
