● FAQ

Frequently asked.

The 28 questions our support team answers most often, grouped.

Which plan should I choose?

Regular license covers a single application — one company, one product. Extended license covers SaaS, multi-tenant resale, and use across multiple end-products. If in doubt, email us before you buy and we'll point you at the right one.

Do you offer custom services & development?

Yes. Each of our top authors takes on 3–6 custom projects per quarter. Typical engagements are 2–8 weeks. Fixed-price or T&M, your call. Submit a brief and we'll respond in 48 hours.

What are your office hours?

Forge is a remote-first team across 4 timezones. Live chat is staffed Mon–Fri, 7am–10pm UTC. Tickets are triaged 24/5 — Sev-1 (production down) tickets get a response within 4 hours, 365 days a year, on Enterprise.

Can I get support for the free builds?

Yes — through our public Discord (link in the footer). Free builds are community-supported; the original author is usually around to help. For commercial support and SLAs, look at the paid editions.

Do your products support RTL languages?

All Forge web themes (HTML, React, Vue) ship with full RTL support out of the box. For backend builds, RTL is irrelevant — all the i18n hooks are in our locale module.

Which Java/Node versions are supported?

Spring Boot builds: Java 17, 21, 23 (LTS lines). Node builds: Node 20, 22. Older versions may work; we don't run CI against them.

Are updates free after I buy?

Yes — lifetime free updates. Both minor (10.x → 10.y) and major (10.x → 11.x) version updates are included with every paid license, forever.

How will I be notified of new updates?

Email on every release, plus an in-product changelog. Our authors typically ship 4–8 updates per build, per year.

What's the refund policy?

14-day, no-questions refund for any reason. After 14 days, we'll consider refunds on a case-by-case basis if a build doesn't deliver what we promised on its product page.

Can I get a refund if a bug isn't fixed?

If a critical bug is reported and not addressed within 14 days, you can request a full refund — even past the 14-day window. We track this and it's reflected in author ratings on the marketplace.

Is the code obfuscated or DRM'd?

No. Every Forge build ships as plain, readable source code via a Git repository invite. No license keys, no phone-home, no anti-tamper. We trust paying customers.

Can I use Forge builds in client projects?

Yes, on the Extended license. A single Extended license covers any number of client deployments of a single end-product. For agency/multi-client work, talk to us — we'll often issue a custom agency license.

Do you offer team / company seats?

Yes. Team licenses are available for any build at 1.5× the single-license price for up to 10 seats, and at custom pricing for 11+. Volume discount kicks in at 20 seats.

Will you migrate my legacy app onto a Forge build?

Yes — that's actually one of the most common custom engagements. Typical migration from a legacy auth stack to Sentry API takes 2–3 weeks of an author's time.
